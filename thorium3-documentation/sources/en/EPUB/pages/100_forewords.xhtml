Forewords

Thorium is a digital book reader, it is also a library where you can store your books and access to online catalogs.

Thorium allows to read ebooks in different formats, each of them having strengths and limitations. This documentation is provided as an EPUB, the format with the larger number of functionalities.

This software is free, open-source and as such, its development is only possible because it is funded by different organizations. Among these, members of EDRLab, private or public grants and small recurring donations from happy users (individuals or companies). If you rely on this application, consider support!
